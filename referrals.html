Referral FAQs

When do I get my reward?

You'll receive your $50 credit automatically after your referral's first paid month is complete. Credits can be used toward your subscription or saved for future use.

Is there a limit to referrals?

No limits! You can refer as many people as you'd like. Each successful referral earns you $50 in credit.

Can I refer existing customers?

The referral program is designed for new customers only. Referrals must be new to Boss Booker and subscribe to a paid plan.

How do I track my referrals?

Once you're a Boss Booker customer, you can track all your referrals and rewards in your account dashboard under the "Referrals" section.
